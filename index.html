Our rescues have grown at an extreme rate since we began. 14 000 rescues were completed in 1992, and this grew to 38 000 in 1997, after only a decade of serving. These numbers contiue to grow, as we recieve almost 1 000 requests for help everyday during the seasons of summer and spring. Not only this, but during the 2018/19 fincial year we rescued over 90 000 animals, and we expect this to be a lot higher this year due to the natural disasters destroying the habbits of our native wildlife.
