Here at WIRES we are Australia's largest wildlife rescue orginisation

Our jorney begins in 1985 when we noticed an obvious need for a wildlife rescue service. Before we were able to launch, no organisation,government or conservation group would take responsibility for the rescue and care of Australian animals. We were fortuate enough to coordinate a variety of rescuers and carers, and officaly launched as an orginisation in March of 1986.
History
Our mission at WIRES is to actively rehabilitate and preserve the Australian wildlife and inspire others to do the same
Not only do we help hundreds of thounsads of comunity members, we provide advice and rescue assistance to the RSPCA, zoos, governmet, councils, police and numerous businesses. WIRES also offers extsive wildlife educational services, through our heavy online presence. We host talks at schools and a number to comunity events to encourage people to get involved and help keep our native animals safe.
